## Why This Work Matters for the Demo

This is the **anchor demo work** because it shows the rarest thing: a GPI dealer record (Goupil, 1892) that matches the AIC provenance prose *by name and year*. The paper trail from Paris dealer to Chicago collector to museum is traceable.

AIC text: *"Goupil et Cie, Paris; sold to Bertha Honoré Palmer (Mrs. Potter Palmer), Chicago, 1892."*
GPI record: Goupil & Cie, April 1891, Degas, 2,250 francs.

These are the same transaction — the first time they've been displayed side by side.
