## Why This Work Matters

Van Gogh's brother **Theo** worked at [[Goupil & Cie]] Paris branch until 1891 — the same year he died. The connection: Theo sold Impressionist works (Degas, Monet, Pissarro) through Goupil while supporting his brother financially. The GPI Goupil records include Theo's transactions on one side; Vincent's paintings are the other side of that same story.

This work has **one of the most complete documented chains** in the collection — useful as a contrast to show users what "well-documented" looks like vs a gap.
